## What it is
**SPAN** is a cross-platform desktop application for interactive analysis and visualization of speech production data. It is designed primarily for **electromagnetic articulography (EMA)** research, integrating synchronized articulatory kinematics, acoustic signals, and gesture-based annotations within a single, unified interface.

SPAN supports exploratory data analysis, manual and semi-automatic annotation, and template-based gesture alignment using Dynamic Time Warping (DTW). The tool is built to facilitate research on speech motor control, articulatory phonology, and speech perceptionâ€“production links, while remaining flexible enough for teaching, demonstrations, and method development.

## Highlights
